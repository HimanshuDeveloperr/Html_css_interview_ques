
When should we use cookies?

Cookies are used in web development for various purposes. Here's a brief overview of when to use cookies:

- Session Management: Use cookies to manage user sessions and maintain login state.
- User Preferences: Cookies can remember user preferences, such as language or theme choices.
- Tracking and Analytics: Cookies help track user behavior and collect analytics data.
- Shopping Carts and E-commerce: Cookies are used to store information about selected products in shopping carts.
- Advertisements and Marketing: Cookies enable targeted advertising based on user interests.
- Cross-Site Communication: Cookies facilitate data exchange between related websites or subdomains.

Remember to consider privacy and provide options for cookie management and consent to comply with regulations and respect user privacy preferences.
